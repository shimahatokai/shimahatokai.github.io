新概念每日一课 007

Lesson 7 Bats

单词

  • strictly (L. 2) adverb, in all details; exactly. 完全。

  • obstruction (L. 5) noun, [countable] something that blocks a road, an entrance, etc. 障碍物（具体的）。

    obstacle (L. 14) noun, a situation, an event, etc. that makes it difficult for you to do or achieve something. 阻碍（抽象的）。

  • off (L. 5) adverb, away from a place; at a distance in space or time. 远离。

句子

  • Not all sounds made by animals serve as lanuage … (L. 1)

    serve as, be suitable for a particular use, especially when nothing else is available. 用作。

  • To get a full appreciation of what this means … (L. 3)
    appreciation of something, [uncountable, singular] a full or sympathetic understanding of something, such as a situation or a problem, and of what it involves. 理解。

    estimation, (formal) a judgement or opinion about the value or quality of somebody/something. 见解。

  • … if he shouts in the vicinity of a wall … (L. 4)

    the vicinity, the area around a particular place. 周边。

  • Every solid object will reflect a sound, varying according to the size … (LL. 8 – 9)

    varying, an -ing form of a verb in a clause which have an adverbial meaning, makes what we say or write more formal. 从句中用动词 -ing 形式作副词，言辞更加庄重。

  • It has been found that certain bats … (L. 13)

    老版为：

    A few years ago it was found that certain bats …

  • … they can locate and steer clear of obstacles … (LL. 13 – 14)
    clear, adverb. clear (of something), away from something; not near or touching something. 远离。

    keep/stay/steer clear (of somebody/something), avoid a person or thing because it may cause problems. 避开。

  • This echo-location in bats is often compared with radar, the principle of which is similar. (LL. 14 – 15)

    compare … with …

    1. examine people or things to see how they are similar and how they are differentbe. 比较异同。

    2. similar to somebody/something else, either better or worse. 媲美。

      compare … to …, show or state that somebody/something is similar to somebody/something else. 誉为，比作。

文章

课文选自 Curiosities of animal life，伦敦 Ward Lock & Co. Ltd 1952年第一版，全书224页，有40幅动物简笔画，用讲述动物趣闻来介绍自然史。

作者

Maurice Burton (28 March 1898 – 9 September 1992)，英国动物学家，科普作家。伦敦大学 King’s College 动物学专业毕业后当了三年生物老师，1927 – 1958 供职大英自然历史博物馆。

1961年，他出版 The Elusive Monster 一书论证尼斯湖水怪（Loch Ness Monster）不存在。

背景

Maurice Burton 的书大部分写给青少年读者，普及自然生物知识。Curiosities of animal life 一书是为 5-8年级（Grades 5-8）10 – 13岁的中小学生写的，语言通俗易懂。
