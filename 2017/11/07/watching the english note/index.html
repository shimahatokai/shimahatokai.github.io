中国人懂英语吗

中国学生学了十几二十年英语，考了四级又考了六级还考了研，过了专四又过了专八，左手中级口译右手高级口译，雅思托福 GRE 0.1%，托业 LSAT GMAT 满分，留学英美，世界名校毕业，哪个西方国家没去过，讲起英语来语速比 native speaker 还快，词汇量分分钟秒杀米村，加村，澳村同学，僻词俚语张口就来。但是，他们真的懂英语吗？

请看下面这些日常英语，你们听懂了吗？

Well, I expect we’ll manage somehow. = Yes, certainly, no trouble.


That would be quite helpful. = For Christ’s sake, that should have been done yesterday!


We seem to have a bit of a problem. = There has been a complete and utter disaster.


Oh, really? How interesting! = I don’t believe a word of it, you lying toad.

Or = I’m bored and not really listening but try to be polite.


With the greatest respect = You must be a fool.


With all due respect … = an even greater insult than ‘You must be a fool’.


I’m sure it’s my fault … = It’s your fault!


I’m sure … = the exact opposite of what we say.


Oh, by the way … = Pay attention, this is the primary purpose of our whole conversation.

Englishman（not Walsh, Scottish, nor Irish）讲英语有一个特点，ironic。

showing that you really mean the opposite of what you are saying

名词，irony：

the use of words that say the opposite of what you really mean, often as a joke and with a tone of voice that shows this

不知道这点，可能会听到英伦友人夸自己 ‘Your English is So Good!’ 便有了幻觉。 其实人家心里也许是在说 ‘Oh damn! Total disaster!’。语言背后，是文化。

文化是润物无声的。说得溜，并不代表懂那种语言，如同普通话考了一甲，却未必懂中文一样。不过不懂也没关系，生活依然如此多娇，很多香港人就不会讲普通话。

以上引文请阅读 pp. 281– 282, Kate Fox, Watching the English: The Hidden Rules of English Behaviour, London: Hodder, 2014.
